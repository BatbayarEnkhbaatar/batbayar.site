⚠️ Troubleshooting:

If you see "not-allowed" error:

  • Check that your microphone is connected and working
  • Reload the page and look for a permission popup from Chrome
  • If you see a permission popup, click "Allow"
  • If no popup appears, go to Chrome Settings → Privacy → Site Settings → Microphone and allow this site
  • Then reload this page and try again

If you see "no-speech" error:

  • Speak louder and closer to the microphone
  • Make sure there's no excessive background noise
  • Wait at least 2 seconds before stopping the recording
